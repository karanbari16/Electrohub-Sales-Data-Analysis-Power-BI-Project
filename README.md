📌 Problem Statement: ElectroHub Sales Analysis

ElectroHub, an e-commerce platform dealing with multiple product categories such as electronics, clothing, and home appliances, generates a large volume of sales data. However, the business faces challenges in effectively analyzing this data to gain meaningful insights.

The organization lacks clarity on:

Which products are performing well or underperforming
How sales and profit are trending over time
The impact of discounts on profitability
Customer purchasing patterns and order behavior
Regional (city-wise) sales performance

Due to the absence of a centralized and interactive reporting system, decision-makers struggle to monitor key performance indicators and make data-driven business decisions.

🎯 Objective:

To develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that:

Tracks key metrics such as Sales, Profit, Quantity Sold, and Orders
Identifies top and bottom-performing products
Analyzes sales trends across different time periods
Evaluates the relationship between sales and profit
Provides insights into discount strategies and their impact
Enables dynamic filtering for detailed, user-driven analys




📊 ElectroHub Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

This project is a comprehensive Power BI dashboard built to analyze sales performance for an e-commerce platform, ElectroHub. It provides actionable insights into product performance, customer behavior, and business trends using interactive visualizations.

🔍 Key Features:
Analysis of Top & Bottom 5 products based on Sales, Profit, and Quantity Sold
Time-based sales trends (daily, monthly, quarterly, yearly)
Visualization of Sales vs Profit relationship
Dynamic comparison between two user-selected time periods
Insights into average discounts across categories
Total number of orders overview
Detailed order-level breakdown with filters (Product, Date, Customer ID, Promotion Category)
City-wise sales distribution


🛠️ Tools Used:
Power BI,
Data Modeling,
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
